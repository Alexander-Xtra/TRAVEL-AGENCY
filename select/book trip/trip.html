Booking Steps

1. Traveler Details

Enter full names as on passport, contact email/phone, and special requests (meals, accessibility, etc.).

2. Travel Protection

3. Payment & Confirmation

4. Pre-Trip Check

Flexible holds

Smart documents

Upload passports and vaccine cards securely once; reuse on future bookings with one tap.

Pro Tips to Save & Smooth Your Trip

  • Shift dates by ±2 days to uncover 10–20% lower fares.
  • Bundle hotel + flight for exclusive partner discounts.
  • Choose mid-week departures; airports are quieter and fares are often cheaper.
  • Carry-on only for trips under 7 days—skip queues and baggage fees.

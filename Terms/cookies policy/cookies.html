What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help remember your preferences, keep sessions secure, and enable analytics and marketing features.

How Alex Travel Uses Cookies

We use cookies responsibly to deliver a smoother travel booking experience:

  • Essential cookies: Keep your session, cart, and booking progress intact.
  • Performance cookies: Help us understand how our site is used so we can improve speed and content.
  • Functional cookies: Remember language, currency, and preferences to personalize your visit.
  • Marketing cookies: With your permission, we show relevant travel offers and measure campaign performance.

Third-party Cookies

We may use trusted third parties (analytics and payment partners). These partners may set cookies to enable their features. We do not control the third-party cookie behavior beyond what they disclose.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may reduce functionality (e.g., booking progress or localized pricing).

If you wish to opt-out of marketing cookies, please contact us at privacy@alextravel.com and we will provide guidance.

Cookie Consent

On first visit you may see a cookie banner asking for consent. Accepting helps us improve your experience; you may change your preferences anytime via browser settings or by contacting us.

Questions about cookies? Email privacy@alextravel.com.

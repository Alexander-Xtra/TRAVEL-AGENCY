About Alex Travel

Turning dreams into journeys since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Alex Travel started as a small family-owned agency helping friends and neighbors explore the world. Over the past decade, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted travel partners, serving thousands of travelers worldwide. From safaris in Africa to city tours in Europe, we make every journey unforgettable.

Our Mission

To make travel easy, accessible, and inspiring by offering personalized and affordable packages tailored to every traveler’s needs.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted travel agency worldwide, known for unforgettable journeys and exceptional service.

Milestones

  • 2020 – Alex Travel was founded in Lagos, Nigeria
  • 2021 – Partnered with top airlines & hotels
  • 2022 – Expanded to 10+ countries
  • 2023 – Reached 20,000 happy customers
  • 2024 – Recognized as “Top African Travel Agency”
